Pune

Pune played a crucial role in the Maratha Empire, particularly under the Peshwas.Shaniwar Wada and Lal Mahal are prominent historical landmarks.The city is known for its vibrant cultural traditions, including the Ganesh festival, which was started in Pune by Lokmanya Tilak. Pune is a cradle of knowledge, with renowned educational institutions like Savitribai Phule Pune University and Fergusson College.It's also a hub for Indian classical music, hosting events like the "Savai Gandharva Music Festival".
